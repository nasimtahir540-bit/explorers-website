Ancient Civilizations

The Foundations of Human History

Before the modern world emerged, incredible civilizations laid the groundwork for engineering, writing, law, and philosophy:

  • Ancient Egypt: Masters of architecture, monumental pyramids, and hieroglyphic writing along the Nile.
  • Mesopotamia: The "Cradle of Civilization" which gave us the first written laws and complex cities.
  • The Indus Valley: Early urban geniuses known for sophisticated city planning, drainage systems, and peaceful trade.
  • Ancient Greece: The birthplace of democracy, Western philosophy, theater, and the early Olympic games.
