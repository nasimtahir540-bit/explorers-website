The Rise of Smart Machines

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming how humans create, calculate, and solve some of the world's most complex challenges:

  • Machine Learning: Systems that analyze massive data pools to discover trends and learn without explicit rules.
  • Neural Networks: Computing models inspired by the biology of the human brain to process language and images.
  • Computer Vision: Giving machines the power to look at, identify, and securely process visual data.
  • Generative AI: Creative models capable of brainstorming, translating, coding, and generating artwork on demand.
